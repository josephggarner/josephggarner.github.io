Food Mood (Food Ordering Service)

Technologies used: HTML * CSS * JavaScript * Firebase

Food Mood was a group project which reimagined services such as GrubHub or UberEats. The application allows a user to sign up as a customer, delivery driver, or restaurant owner. Depending on the user's role, they have access to different features of the application. Customers can browse restaurants and their menus in order to fill a cart with items they can then order. Drivers accept orders and drop them off. Owners can manage their restuarant and accept or reject orders placed by customers.
